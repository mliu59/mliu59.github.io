Hello! My name is Miles Liu. I recently graduated from Johns Hopkins University with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering, and I am currently working towards a Master of Science in Engineering in Robotics at the JHU Laboratory for Computational and Sensory Robotics, expecting to graduate in 2022.

I currently work as an Engineering Associate with the amazing team at the startup company ClearMask, LLC, where my main responsibilities include development, testing, and maintenance of new products, external webpages, and internal digital data and operational platforms.
