Engineering Project Portfolio:

Autonomous Vehicle Testing Asset (VTA) (Completed)

  • Mechanical Engineering Senior Design project, sponsored by Oceaneering International, Inc.
  • An autonomous surface vehicle that serves as a tether management system to mitigate tether tension for Oceaneering’s small subsea vehicles during vehicle testing and deployment.
  • Our team was awarded Best Presentation by a panel of ASME judges.

Biaxial Stretching Device for Cell-seeded Biogels (Completed)

  • Nguyen Laboratory for Mechanics of Soft Adaptive Materials
  • A custom biaxial stretcher device for cell-seeded biogels, used for investigating mechanical and structural behavior of astrocyte cells under mechanical straining.

DECON Autonomous Disinfection Unit (Completed)

  • Leading Innovation Design Team project, sponsored by the JHU Applied Physics Laboratory.
  • A mobile autonomous robot platform that identifies and safely disinfects contamination hotspots in healthcare facilities.

SONDER (Ongoing)

  • SONDER is a crowd-sourced experience sharing network to help facilitate connection and experience sharing between university students.
  • The network is built to fill in gaps in university advising by introducing wide range of student perspectives to help inform individual decisions.
